About Me

Graduate in Psychology having a keen interest Industrial Psychology and Human Resource Management, anchoring events, and content writing.

December 19, 2023 marked a significant milestone of me completing 5 years of anchoring & hosting events. I still remember the absolute happiness I felt when I signed off as an Anchor that night!

By now I must have spoken in front of thousands, hosted for the Government of Maharashtra, managed CSR Programs for MNCs, and become a part of numerous families. However, these are just statistics.

What made my five years fabulous was the opportunity to see people genuinely smile, host a baby shower, and then hold the same baby in my arms a year later. Witnessing life transitions from hosting first birthdays to the 100th birthday was incredible, and of course, receiving blessings!

Life's too short for just one gig!

Here's to many more years of creating magical moments and spreading joy through my passion for hosting!

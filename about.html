About Me

A dynamic and spirited individual with a background in psychology, who thrives on connecting with people and spreading joy. With an infectious sense of humour and a knack for lighting up every room, she brings energy and laughter wherever she goes.

December 19, 2023, marked a milestone in my journey as an anchor and event host—5 wonderful years of creating unforgettable moments! I still cherish the joy I felt signing off as an anchor that night, a feeling that continues to fuel my passion for this art.

Over the years, I’ve had the privilege of speaking in front of thousands, hosting events for esteemed brands like Tata Motors, Kidzee, and Schneider, and collaborating with organizations like the Government of Maharashtra. From managing CSR programs for MNCs to becoming part of countless families' celebrations, my journey has been nothing short of incredible.

But beyond the numbers and accolades, it’s the human connections that have truly enriched my experience—seeing genuine smiles, hosting a baby shower and then celebrating the same child's first birthday, and being part of life’s beautiful transitions, from first birthdays to centenarian celebrations.

With a background in psychology, I bring a unique ability to connect with diverse audiences and create moments that matter. Life’s too short for just one gig, and I’m here to light up every stage, event, and gathering with energy, humor, and heartfelt interactions.
